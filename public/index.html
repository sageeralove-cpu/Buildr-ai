✦ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Buildr AI. Can't find what you're looking for? Chat with our AI agent.

Do I need coding experience to use Buildr AI? +
Absolutely not! Buildr AI is designed for everyone, regardless of technical background. Simply describe what you want in plain English, and our AI handles all the coding, design, and deployment. Think of it like having a full development team at your fingertips.
Can I really publish apps directly to the App Store? +
Yes! With our Pro and Business plans, you can publish directly to the Apple App Store and Google Play Store. We handle the build process, code signing, and submission. You just need an Apple Developer account ($99/year) or Google Play Developer account ($25 one-time fee).
How does social media auto-publishing work? +
Connect your social media accounts (TikTok, Instagram, X, Facebook, LinkedIn) by securely linking them in your Buildr AI dashboard. Create content using AI, schedule posts, and publish directly to all platforms simultaneously — no switching between apps.
What AI agents are available? +
Our AI agents include: Build Agent (creates your apps/sites), Design Agent (handles aesthetics and UI), Debug Agent (finds and fixes issues), Content Agent (generates social media content), and Support Agent (answers your questions 24/7). Pro and Business users get priority access with faster response times.
Is there a free plan? +
Yes! Our Starter plan is completely free and gives you 3,000 AI credits to build websites, apps, dashboards, PDFs, and more. Templates are always free and unlimited. When you need more credits, premium features, or app store publishing — upgrade to Pro or Business.
How secure is my data? +
We take security seriously. All data is encrypted at rest and in transit using AES-256 and TLS 1.3. We comply with GDPR, the Australian Privacy Act 1988, CCPA, and international data protection standards. Social media credentials are stored using OAuth 2.0 — we never see your passwords.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime? +
Absolutely. You can cancel your subscription at any time from your dashboard. There are no contracts, no cancellation fees, and no questions asked. Your projects remain accessible even after cancellation, though publishing features will revert to Starter plan limits.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We offer flexible payment options to suit everyone. You can pay with credit or debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover) processed via Stripe, or use PayPal for a familiar checkout experience. We also support Klarna — choose to pay now, pay in 30 days, or split into 4 interest-free instalments. Apple Pay and Google Pay are available for instant mobile and desktop checkout. For Business plans, we additionally offer bank transfer and invoicing options. All payment methods are secured with industry-standard encryption.
